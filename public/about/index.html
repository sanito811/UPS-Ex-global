Who We Are

UPS-Exglobal is a leading international shipping and logistics company headquartered in Phoenix, Arizona. With over 25 years of experience, we've built a reputation for reliability, speed, and exceptional customer service.

We serve customers in over 200 countries and territories, handling millions of packages annually with our state-of-the-art tracking systems and global network of partners.
